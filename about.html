ABOUT US






Wisdom360 is a holistic coaching platform that caters to fulfilling the educational and career aspirations of students.
We understand that every age group has its own learning needs. Therefore, we tutor primary-school children to higher education aspirants in such a way that learning becomes a ‘life-long process’.
An initiative by IIT & IIM Alumni, Wisdom360 has established various programs for Class 2 to 12 – blending objective/subjective and offline/online learning methods. There are also special workshops for the purposes of student orientation and Career Counselling.

Bringing our full-rounded educational techniques to use, we also render :

  • Preparation classes for several entrance exams after 12th or 10th class, so that no student misses out on pursuing the courses or colleges he/she aspires for Coaching classes for talent search exams like NTSE and various Olympiads
  • A Global Program to prepare students for international boards
  • Profile Building Programs through various in-house clubs that enhance a student’s ability to ace brain sports based on Vedic Math, Coding for kids
  • Kid-pre-neurship, Music, Language (Spelling Bee), and more!

MISSION

To create a stress-free tutorial space where innovative learning methods are encouraged in such a way that obtaining excellent marks becomes a by-product of the student’s inherent genius.





VISION

To make tutoring for school, higher education and competitive exams accessible and affordable while putting the best teaching practices in use, reducing the stress on students, and making their subject base strong.





OBJECTIVE FOR
THE COMPANY

To enable the students in
their educational journey :



  • Self-learning techniques
  • Thoroughly curated study material
  • Focused execution strategies
  • Consistent counseling support
  • One-to-one doubt-clearing sessions and feedbacks
  • An ideal student-teacher ratio
  • Online support, smart classes, and first-rate infrastructural support

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

ABOUT THE DIRECTOR

Dr. Upendra Tripathi considers himself as a lifelong learner. His passion to enable children and youngsters with the power of independent thinking and an excellent learning attitude lead to the conception of Wisdom360. Through Wisdom360, he has been able to create an equitable educational culture for our next generation wherein students are encouraged to ask questions and teachers are encouraged to listen.
Due to Dr. Tripathi’s earnest desire for an all-round development of students, Wisdom360 ended up becoming a long-standing pioneer of delivering short-term profile building courses. These courses are conducted under the watchful eye of esteemed mentors who are ex-alumni of world-class institutions.
A believer in the philosophy – “Try new things irrespective of the results”, Dr. Tripathi ensures that Wisdom360 doesn’t get stuck in conventional learning practices. Though a great leader and team player, he considers his students as his extended family and envisions for them a future wherein they not only know ‘what to think’, but also ‘how to think’. Teaching such independent and critical thinking skills is of utmost importance at Wisdom360 and therefore, percolates through its every tutoring program - starting from the ones custom-made for dreamers in primary school to achievers in higher institutions.
Keeping up with Dr. Tripathi’s longing to ‘do more and learn more’, the Wisdom360 family has also launched UDAAN, a social and humanitarian initiative that focuses on providing education to the most underprivileged and neglected talents of our country.
Dr. Upendra Tripathi completed his Bachelors in Engineering from HBTI Kanpur and went on to earn a PhD degree from IIT Delhi. He has served as Head of Department and Centre Head in some of the leading institutes of Delhi and Lucknow.
