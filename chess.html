WE TAKE A MULTI-LEVEL APPROACH TO PROMOTING AND TEACHING THE GAME OF CHESS

Our Scholastic Outreach approach spreads the educational benefits of chess to students

Provides quality lessons to the students instead of focusing on the quantity

Experts provide proper curriculum and pattern to progress in the game

Unique Training plan which helps students work in the right direction and acquire complete knowledge of the game

Curriculum has a collection of useful positions and games to coach our students to learn in simplified manner

Regular homework is given to the students for practicing the concepts taught in class and it is checked by our coaches in the next class

Limited batch size (Maximum of 8 in online and 20 in offline)
