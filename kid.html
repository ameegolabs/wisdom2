AGE (13 - 19)

Our program at Wisdom360 guides kids through the steps of building their own business while introducing them to the exciting world of entrepreneurship and enhancing their workplace readiness, regardless of future career choices. Children are introduced to the world of entrepreneurship in a fun and engaging way – by starting their own business! Kids learn all the business basics, along with valuable life skills, as they prepare to sell their products or service.
