NURTURE

CLASS (I-IV)





We have taken special care while devising the Nurture program. Middle childhood (Age 5 to 9) is when students start refining their motor skills, test new academic skills by accessing their abilities and accomplishments and making comparisons between self and others. It was, therefore, very important for us to look into the following:

  • • Which methods of teaching are the most favourable for this age group?
  • • How to ensure that learning techniques suit every child’s individual needs?
  • • Which tools and methods promote ‘learning by observation’ instead of rote learning?
So how do we understand the personalized needs of each child?

At Wisdom360, we perform DMIT tests (a type of aptitude and intelligence test) which helps us understand the unique characteristics of all children. We then segregate the little ones into groups and customize our learning tools accordingly.
Instead of teaching through books, we empower a child with ‘experiential learning’ i.e. learning by doing! We make this possible by:

  • • Keenly observing a child
  • • Making him or her learning his own way at a steady pace
  • • Making him or her deliver results by posing challenges
  • • Let him or her ask questions and seek answers

    • Parents must note that, under the Nurture Program, all subjects are covered for respective boards – CBSE, ICSE, IB and others. In short, this program can accommodate all national and international boards.
